How engagements can work

Focused support from question to decision.

  1. Clarify the decision. Frame the evaluation or analysis around the action stakeholders need to take.
  2. Design the evidence plan. Match methods, data, timelines, and constraints to the question.
  3. Analyze and interpret. Produce rigorous results with transparent assumptions and usable outputs.
  4. Communicate for use. Convert findings into reports, workshops, manuscripts, or decision memos.
